Online Coding Classes – Level 2 and Level 3

If your child is ready to master a new programming skill, signing up for a Mastery Pack is great way to get them starting on their coding adventure!

We have bundled the three levels of a particular programming language so it can be purchased at once with a 20% off discount! Our Mastery Packs allow you to sign up for the class and let your child continue through each level without checking schedules, availability, or signing up again. The convenience and cost savings is a big hit with families looking for a focused coding program so their child to learn, create, and master a programming skill.

Whether your child wants to design their own video game, create web pages, develop animations, or learn about real-world programming languages, we have a Level 1-3 Mastery Pack for them!
