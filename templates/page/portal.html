Additional Enrichment

There will be some additional enrichment exercises your child can complete after each lesson. These will be listed in the Advantage portal after the first class is completed.

General Tips
  • For the first class, we recommend an adult stays near the student for the first few minutes to help them get set up and maximize class time.
  • Your child can log into the Advantage portal anytime; however, our online classroom will open 5 minutes prior to class.
  • Upon completion of the week’s class, ask your child to show you what they learned and did in the class. Our students learn new things and are often excited to share.
  • Their project will be saved on their computer so they may go back and further explore/build upon the lesson at a later time.

Additional enrichment is optional but students should be encouraged to do it every week if they have an interest. It is meant to reinforce what they have learned in class and offer additional challenges.

If your child has questions, they can log in 5 min prior to the start of the next class to ask their instructor.
To access the additional enrichment exercises, simply enter the Advantage Portal. Your log in (which was sent in an email confirming your child’s registration) will remain active for 1 week after the last class.

Classroom policies
  • Kids must wear headphones on for the duration of the class.
  • There is limited opportunity to catch up a child who enters the classroom 10min after the start time.
  • Instructor will log on 5 min prior to class start to answer any questions about homework or if students want to show anything they have done.
  • Kids must be respectful to the instructor and classmates. If there are consistent behavior issues, a parent conference will be scheduled to address
  • There are NO make-ups for any classes missed.
  • There will be additional learning enrichment available in our Advantage Portal after each lesson so a child can go beyond the lesson and challenge themselves.
Enrollment policy

This class may be cancelled if minimum enrollment requirements are not met.
Notice will go out 1 week prior in the event we need to cancel the class.
If a child is repeatedly disruptive or disrespectful in class, either to the teacher or their classmates, we reserve the right to remove the student from class, refunding any remaining classes. Parents will be notified of such behavior concerns before any actions are taken.

Technical Difficulties

If you are experiencing any difficulties logging into class or during the class, please let your instructor know. If they are not able to solve with issue without distracting from the class (already in session), send an email to IT@codeadvantage.org so that we can work with you outside of class time to resolve the issue.
NOTE: If any changes have been made to the computer or equipment after the tech assessment, please let us know at IT@codeadvantage.org so that we may avoid any unexpected issues during class.
