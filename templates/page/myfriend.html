Me and My Friends

10,000+ KIDS HAVE LEARNED TO CODE WITH US | 40+ ROCKSTAR INSTRUCTORS | 100% LIVE  SMALL GROUP CLASSES |  35 COURSES FOR AGES 6 - 13!

Get the gang together again, in a LIVE online coding class!

Uncertainty is the norm, these days. But, as parents, we are persevering and putting in place new educational pathways to help to offset the impact on our children. CodeAdvantage is a great fit for your child's learning journey because even amidst the shifting environment, we are very certain of two things:

  1. Children love spending time with their friends.

  2. Friends love learning together.

Learning pod? Remote Schooling? Homeschooling? Or, maybe a hybrid? We've got you covered!

Whatever your education plan looks like, this year, My Friends Online LIVE coding classes can help to satisfy your child's needs for personal connection, enrichment, and growth. My Friends Online allows you to curate an online learning pod with your child and his or her group of friends. You pick the learners, class, day, time, and frequency. We provide the Rockstar Instructor, an exciting project-based coding curriculum, and the fun!

My Friends Online will check the box on most of your goals for extracurricular activities and enrichment.

  • Supplement your child’s education with critical STEM/Coding skills not often taught in schools.

  • Find flexible online coding classes that work your family’s schedule whether you child is remote schooling, in a learning pod, or homeschooling.

  • Have fun with friends while learning from talented, experienced, and engaging LIVE instructors

How to Launch a My Friends Online Class!

1

Select the class your child wants to take. Decide on frequency, day(s), and class time(s). Classes can run once a week, twice a week, or even every day of the week in one or two hour blocks. You will be planning for a total of ten hours of instruction.

2

Email the parents of your child's friends! Share the link to this page as well as the classes you are considering, and the schedule you are shooting for. Classes can accommodate up to seven students.

3

When you have the students and schedule firmed up, click on the registration link below and complete our online form! Our Customer Service Team will reply with an email confirming all the details of your class and instructor availability. After that, we will send registration emails and invoices to the parents of the learners in your My Friends Online class!
