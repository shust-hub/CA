Awesome LIVE Online Coding Classes for Kids

In this time of education uncertainty, CodeAdvantage provides a vibrant learning path where children look forward to their classes, opt into extra enrichment activities, and create exciting new things with code!

Through LIVE online instruction and a project-based curriculum, our learners have fun, connect, and grow. And, with choices for small group and private online classes, CodeAdvantage can fit into what ever learning path you have chosen this year—remote schooling, learning pods, homeschooling, or even a hybrid approach.

CONSIDER US YOUR ONLINE CODING CLASS CONCIERGE

Three ways to get exactly what you're looking for.

  1. Need to fill a slot in the schedule? Register for one of our many weekly or daily classes. On some days, we run more than 100 classes across many time zones. Receive discounted rates depending on the class time you pick

  2. Want your child to spend more time with friends? Select our My Friends Online offering, where we create classes for a custom pod of your child's friends at the day and time of your choosing

  3. Looking for a deep dive? Schedule some 1:1 time with one of our Rockstar instructors. Pick an off-peak time and receive discounted rates.
